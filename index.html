About the Conference

A space built for women who educate

Anchor Her is a conference for women who teach at the high school level where the conversations that matter most often go unspoken. From supporting pregnant students to addressing bullying and mental health, this is space to speak openly, learn together, and leave better equipped.

Join fellow high school educators for two hours of honest, necessary conversation about the real challenges students face and how teachers can lead the response with confidence and care.

This is just the beginning. Anchor Her is designed to become a recurring event held once or twice a year as a dedicated space for women teachers to come together, share knowledge, support one another, and grow as a community.
