About the Event

A focused summit for future-ready futures

AnchorHer Summit is a focused, high-impact gathering bringing together educators, innovators, and community leaders to address one of Europe's most urgent challenges: supporting girls and young mothers to stay in education and access future-ready skills.

Built by Vetify Oy, this summit explores how Finland's proven education model can inspire scalable solutions for a rapidly changing, AI-driven world.

This is not just an education issue, it's a future workforce challenge. AnchorHer is the beginning of a new ecosystem designed to keep education alive and futures on track.
